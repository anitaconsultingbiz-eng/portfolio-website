About Me

I build structure behind the scenes.

I spent more than 20 years in higher education supporting graduate programs, operations, and student success initiatives. In recent years, I’ve also worked with nonprofits and consulting clients on websites, systems, curriculum projects, and operational improvements.

Much of my work has involved stepping into environments where strong ideas existed, but the structure, workflows, or operational systems needed to support the work were still evolving.

In Higher Education, I supported graduate business programs across advising, online operations, enrollment planning, student success initiatives, and institutional projects. I also developed systems for enrollment forecasting, onboarding, and academic support workflows.

In nonprofit and community-based organizations, I’ve helped build websites, organize grant and reporting systems, support curriculum development, and translate complex subject matter into practical learning experiences.

I enjoy turning scattered information into organized systems that help people work more clearly and effectively.
